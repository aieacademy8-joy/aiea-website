# **Intellectual Property & Permissions**

## **Building AI Literacy Together**

The mission of **AI Explorers Academy™** is to help children, families, educators, and schools navigate the age of Artificial Intelligence with wisdom, creativity, critical thinking, and responsibility.

We believe knowledge grows when it is shared, and innovation grows when creators are respected. Our goal is not to limit learning, but to preserve the integrity of educational work so it can continue benefiting children around the world.

To support both collaboration and academic integrity, we ask that our original work be used respectfully.

## **The AI Explorers Compass™**

The **AI Explorers Compass™** is the signature educational framework of AI Explorers Academy™, created by **Irene Dostov (Miss JOY)**.

The framework reflects years of educational experience, curriculum design, research, and practical classroom implementation. It represents the educational philosophy that guides all AI Explorers Academy™ programs, publications, and learning resources.

The AI Explorers Compass™ and its associated educational materials are protected as the intellectual property of their creator.

## **Educational Use**

We warmly encourage educators, researchers, schools, and organizations to learn from and reference our work.

You are welcome to:

* Reference the AI Explorers Compass™ in presentations and professional discussions.  
* Discuss the framework during workshops and conferences.  
* Quote short excerpts from our books and educational materials with proper attribution.  
* Cite the framework in academic papers, research, and publications.  
* Share links to our official website and resources.  
* Recommend our books and educational materials to families, educators, and schools.

## **Attribution**

Whenever possible, please provide appropriate credit.

For example:

**Based on the AI Explorers Compass™ by Miss JOY (Irene Dostov), Founder of AI Explorers Academy™**

or

**Source: AI Explorers Academy™ — AI Explorers Compass™**

Academic citations are always appreciated and help strengthen a growing global community committed to responsible AI Literacy.

## **Commercial Use**

Commercial use of the AI Explorers Compass™ or other AI Explorers Academy™ intellectual property requires written permission.

This includes, but is not limited to:

* Teacher training programs  
* Paid workshops  
* Consulting services  
* Curriculum licensing or resale

If you are interested in licensing the framework or partnering with AI Explorers Academy™, we would be delighted to hear from you.

## **Translations**

We are honored that educators around the world may wish to translate and share our work.

To preserve the integrity and consistency of the AI Explorers Compass™ and AI Explorers Academy™ resources, please contact us before publishing translated versions.

**Our long-term vision is to make these resources available in multiple languages through official editions and trusted educational partnerships.** This helps ensure that every translation accurately reflects the original educational philosophy, terminology, and educational intent.

## **Books & Publications**

Reasonable quotations from our books are welcome with appropriate attribution.

Large portions of text, illustrations, downloadable resources, worksheets, or other Academy materials may not be reproduced, republished, or redistributed without written permission.

Thank you for helping us build a future where AI Literacy is accessible, ethical, and rooted in integrity.
