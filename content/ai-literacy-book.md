\# Overview

Artificial Intelligence is changing childhood faster than any previous technology.

Children are already learning, creating, searching, and communicating alongside AI.

Yet most parents, teachers, and schools still ask the same questions:

• Where do we begin?

• What should children actually know?

• How much AI is appropriate?

• What skills matter most?

• How do we prepare children for jobs that don't exist yet?

AI Literacy for Children was written to answer those questions.

Rather than focusing on specific AI tools \- which constantly change \- the book introduces timeless principles that help children think critically, create responsibly, and develop healthy relationships with artificial intelligence.

The goal is not to make every child an AI engineer.

The goal is to help every child become an informed, confident, and responsible citizen in an AI-powered world.

\# Featured Framework

\#\# The AI Explorers Compass™

One of the book's central frameworks is the AI Explorers Compass™, developed specifically to help children build lifelong AI Literacy.

The Compass develops five essential capacities:

🧭 Awareness

🎨 Creation

🧠 Critical Thinking
