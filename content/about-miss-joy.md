\# Meet Miss JOY

\#\# Preparing the First AI-Native Generation \- with Wisdom, Creativity, and Purpose

Irene Dostov, professionally known as \*\*Miss JOY\*\*, is an AI Literacy Educator, Curriculum Architect, international speaker, author, and the founder of \*\*AI Explorers Academy™\*\*.

Her work focuses on one of education's newest challenges:

\*\*How do we prepare children for a world where artificial intelligence becomes part of everyday life?\*\*

While many conversations about AI focus on technology, Irene believes the future belongs to children who can think critically, create responsibly, and remain deeply human while working alongside AI.

That belief became the foundation of AI Explorers Academy™.

\---

\# My Mission

Every child deserves to understand AI, not simply use it.

Artificial Intelligence is changing how children learn, create, communicate, and solve problems.

Preparing children for this future requires more than introducing new digital tools.

It requires developing wisdom, curiosity, creativity, ethical judgment, and the confidence to think independently.

My mission is to help families, educators, and schools build those lifelong abilities through age-appropriate AI Literacy education.

\---

\# Why I Created AI Explorers Academy™

After spending more than a decade working in international education, I began asking a simple question:

\> \*\*If today's children will grow up alongside AI, who will teach them how to think with it?\*\*

I discovered that most educational programs focused on teaching AI tools.

Others focused primarily on coding.

Very few focused on helping children understand AI, question AI, use AI responsibly, and develop the uniquely human abilities that artificial intelligence cannot replace.

So I decided to build what I wished already existed.

AI Explorers Academy™ was created to provide practical, age-appropriate AI Literacy education for parents, teachers, schools, and children from early childhood through primary education.

\---

\# My Educational Philosophy

Technology will continue to evolve.

Human character must evolve with it.

At AI Explorers Academy™, AI Literacy is not about becoming dependent on artificial intelligence.

It is about helping children become thoughtful, creative, ethical, and confident individuals who know when \- and when not \- to use AI.

Our goal is not to prepare children to compete against AI.

Our goal is to prepare them to think with AI.

\---

\# The AI Explorers Compass™

To support this vision, I created the \*\*AI Explorers Compass™\*\*—a practical educational framework designed specifically for children.

Rather than teaching disconnected AI tools, the Compass develops five lifelong capacities:

🧭 Awareness

Helping children recognize where AI exists in everyday life and understand how it works.

🎨 Creation

Using AI as a creative partner while keeping human imagination at the center of every project.

🧠 Critical Thinking

Teaching children to question AI-generated information, verify facts, recognize limitations, and think independently.

❤️ Ethics & Responsibility

Helping young learners make thoughtful, responsible, and human-centered decisions when using AI.

⭐ Human Flourishing

Developing empathy, communication, resilience, purpose, and the uniquely human qualities that technology cannot replace.

The AI Explorers Compass™ serves as the foundation for every Academy curriculum, book, classroom resource, and family learning experience.
