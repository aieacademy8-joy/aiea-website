\# AI Safety & Privacy

\#\# Our Commitment

AI Explorers Academy™ is committed to creating safe, age-appropriate, and responsible AI learning experiences for children.

Child safety, privacy, and ethical AI use are foundational principles of the Academy—not afterthoughts. As our curriculum, pilot programs, and school partnerships continue to develop, our detailed implementation policies are being refined alongside them.

We believe children should learn about Artificial Intelligence in environments that prioritize wellbeing, thoughtful guidance, and responsible digital citizenship.

\---

\#\# Our Current Approach

The Academy follows the principle that children's interaction with AI should always be:

• age-appropriate

• developmentally appropriate

• supervised by trusted adults or educators

• aligned with responsible AI practices

• respectful of children's privacy and wellbeing

Whenever possible, AI should support learning while preserving children's independent thinking, creativity, and human relationships.

\---

\#\# Formal Academy Policies

As implementation expands, AI Explorers Academy™ will publish comprehensive Academy policies covering:

• Approved AI tools and platforms

• Minimum age recommendations

• Direct versus teacher-mediated AI use

• Student data privacy procedures

• Parent and guardian consent

• Child protection standards

• School implementation guidelines

These policies will continue evolving alongside educational research, international best practices, and responsible AI development.

\---

\#\# Our Guiding Principle

Children should learn about AI in ways that protect their privacy, support their wellbeing, encourage critical thinking, and promote ethical and responsible technology use.

Technology should never come before the child.
