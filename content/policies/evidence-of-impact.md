\# Evidence of Impact

\#\# Our Commitment to Evidence-Based Education

AI Explorers Academy™ believes educational innovation should be supported by meaningful evidence rather than marketing claims.

Our goal is not simply to measure participation, but to understand how children develop AI Literacy, critical thinking, creativity, ethical reasoning, and responsible decision-making over time.

\---

\#\# Current Status

AI Explorers Academy™ is entering its implementation phase through educational partnerships, pilot programs, curriculum deployment, and professional learning initiatives.

As these programs grow, we will systematically document their impact using both qualitative and quantitative evidence.

\---

\#\# What We Will Measure

Evidence of impact will be collected through multiple sources, including:

• Educator feedback

• Parent testimonials

• Student project portfolios

• Classroom observations

• Learning reflections

• AI Literacy competency growth

• School implementation outcomes

• Longitudinal observations where appropriate

\---

\#\# Future Publications

As our work expands, we plan to publish:

• Case studies

• Pilot program reports

• Partner school implementation stories

• Examples of student learning

• Educator experiences

• Family feedback

• Research-informed impact summaries

Whenever possible, findings will be presented using transparent methodologies and clearly documented evidence.

\---

\#\# Commitment to Transparency

\*\*AI Explorers Academy™ does not publish testimonials, statistics, or outcome claims that cannot be independently verified.\*\*

As evidence becomes available, it will be shared openly, accurately, and responsibly to support informed decision-making by educators, schools, families, and educational partners.

Our commitment is to earn trust through evidence—not marketing.
