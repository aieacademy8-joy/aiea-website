Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages is this journey designed for?

It is designed primarily for families with children ages 5–9. Younger children may participate with more parent guidance, while older siblings can join the discussions and challenges.

Does my child need to use an AI tool?

No. Several missions are screen-light or completely screen-free. The goal is to build foundational thinking habits before focusing on specific tools.

Do parents need previous AI experience?

Not at all. Every mission includes simple explanations, discussion questions, and guided activities that parents and children can complete together.

How long does the journey take?

Your family can complete one mission each day, one each week, or follow your own rhythm. It is designed to fit naturally into family life rather than feel like additional homework.

What will I receive?

You will receive a 17-page digital PDF containing seven illustrated family missions, activities, conversation prompts, colorable badges, a Family Promise, and a final celebration experience.

Is this a physical product?

No. This is an instant digital download. No physical item will be shipped.

May I print it?

Yes. Your purchase includes permission to print one copy for use within your immediate household.

Can teachers, churches, groups, or schools use it?

The family license is intended for one household. Teachers, churches, groups, and schools should contact AI Explorers Academy for educational or institutional licensing.

What materials will we need?

Most activities require only simple household materials such as paper, pencils, crayons, or a device when a mission includes optional AI exploration.

What is the refund policy?

Because this is an instantly accessible digital product, purchases are generally non-refundable. If you experience a technical problem receiving or opening the file, please contact us for support.
