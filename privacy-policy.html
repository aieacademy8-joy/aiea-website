Privacy

Privacy Policy

AI Explorers Academy™ respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact information you voluntarily provide
  • Information submitted through contact or inquiry forms
  • Basic website analytics (such as pages visited and browser type)

We do not collect payment card information directly on our website. Payments are securely processed through trusted third-party payment providers such as Stripe.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Deliver requested resources
  • Send educational updates (when you have opted in)
  • Improve our website and educational services

We do not sell your personal information.

Children's Privacy

AI Explorers Academy™ creates educational resources for children, but our website is intended for parents, educators, schools, and other adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze website performance. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Our website may link to trusted third-party services including payment processors, social media platforms, and educational tools. Each service has its own privacy practices.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact Miss JOY, Founder, AI Explorers Academy™ — missjoy@aiexplorersacademy.org.
