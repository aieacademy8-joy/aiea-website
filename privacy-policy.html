Privacy & Child Safety

Privacy Policy

Children should learn about AI in ways that protect their privacy, support their wellbeing, and promote responsible technology use.

Being finalized

A comprehensive Privacy Policy is being developed alongside the Academy's curriculum, pilot programs, and school partnerships. In the meantime, the principles below describe our commitment to child safety, privacy, and responsible AI use.

Our Commitment

AI Explorers Academy™ is committed to creating safe, age-appropriate, and responsible AI learning experiences for children. Child safety, privacy, and ethical AI use are foundational principles of the Academy — not afterthoughts.

Our Current Approach

The Academy follows the principle that children's interaction with AI should always be:

  • age-appropriate and developmentally appropriate
  • supervised by trusted adults or educators
  • aligned with responsible AI practices
  • respectful of children's privacy and wellbeing

Whenever possible, AI should support learning while preserving children's independent thinking, creativity, and human relationships.

Formal Academy Policies

As implementation expands, AI Explorers Academy™ will publish comprehensive policies covering approved AI tools and platforms, minimum age recommendations, direct versus teacher-mediated AI use, student data privacy procedures, parent and guardian consent, child protection standards, and school implementation guidelines. These policies will continue evolving alongside educational research, international best practices, and responsible AI development.

Our Guiding Principle

Children should learn about AI in ways that protect their privacy, support their wellbeing, encourage critical thinking, and promote ethical and responsible technology use. Technology should never come before the child.

For privacy questions or requests, please contact missjoy@aiexplorersacademy.org.
