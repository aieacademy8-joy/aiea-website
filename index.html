Our Mission

Preparing the First AI-Native Generation

Generation Alpha and Generation Beta are the first children to grow up alongside Artificial Intelligence.

Every child deserves to understand AI — not simply use it.

AI Explorers Academy helps children, families, educators, and schools develop AI Literacy through curiosity, creativity, critical thinking, ethics, and human flourishing — so children learn to think with AI, not depend on it.

The future belongs to those who can think critically, create responsibly, and collaborate intelligently with AI.
New to AI Literacy?

Start With the Book

AI is already part of childhood — but most parents, educators, and school leaders are still asking the same question: Where do we begin?

AI Literacy for Children offers a practical starting point for understanding what children need in an AI-powered world — without chasing every new tool or platform.

Written for the adults shaping childhood, the book explores the questions, decisions, and human capacities that matter most as Artificial Intelligence becomes part of everyday life.

By Irene DostovFounder, AI Explorers Academy™

  • What children actually need to understand about AI
  • Why AI Literacy is different from learning AI tools
  • How AI is reshaping learning and childhood
